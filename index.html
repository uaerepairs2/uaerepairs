Common Water Pump Problems Faced by Dubai Residents

Water pump issues can develop due to regular usage, heat exposure, poor maintenance, or technical faults. Some of the most common problems include:

  • Low or inconsistent water pressure
  • Pump not turning on
  • Strange noise or vibration
  • Overheating of the pump motor
  • Water leakage from the pump body or pipes
  • Electrical faults or wiring issues
  • Worn-out impellers, seals, or bearings
